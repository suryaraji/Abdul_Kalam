Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, known as A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, was a renowned Indian scientist and the 11th President of India. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India, and passed away on July 27, 2015.

Dr. Kalam was a visionary leader and played a crucial role in India's space and missile development programs. He was also known as the "People's President" due to his deep connection with the youth and his efforts to inspire and educate them.

This website is a tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, celebrating his contributions to science, education, and his dedication to the betterment of society.
